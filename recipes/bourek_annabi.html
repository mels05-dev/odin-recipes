Bourek Annabi

Home

Annaba bourek is an Algerian specialty originating from the city of Annaba . It is a savory appetizer made with brik pastry , filled with mashed potatoes and a mixture of minced meat or tuna, onions, cheese, and parsley. A seasoned raw egg is topped and cooked once the brik pastry is folded into a square and dipped in boiling oil.

Ingredients:

  • 0.9 Lb Minced meat
  • 2 Medium Potatoes
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper
  • Finely chopped fresh parsley
  • Cheese
  • Sliced green olives
  • Caper
  • 1 Egg per person
  • Brick Pastry sheets

Steps

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes,season with salt and pepper to taste and set aside to cool.
  2. Cook the minced meat over medium heat,add the finely chopped onio and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. cook until the meat is nicely browned and the onion is soft .
  4. At the end of the cooking add grated cheese and stir in finely chopped parsley.
  5. Remove from the heat and let the filling cool completely
  6. Heat a generous amount of oil in a deep skillet or fryer over medium-high heat.The oil should be hot but not smoking.
  7. Place a brick pastry sheet on a clean work surface or plate.
  8. Lay half a brick sheet in the center to reinforce the base and help prevent leaks.
  9. Spread 1 tablespoon of mashed potatoes in the center.
  10. Top with 2 tablespoons of the meat filling.
  11. Sprinkle with a few capers, some chopped green onions, and a little grated cheese if using.
  12. Gently make a small well in the center of the filling and carefully crack an egg into it.
  13. Carefully fold the brick pastry to fully enclose the filling, making sure it is well sealed.
  14. Gently lower the bourek into the hot oil and fry on both sides until they are golden brown and perfectly crispy.
  15. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels before serving.
  16. Place the bourek on paper towels to remove any excess oil.
  17. Serve immediately, while still hot and crispy, ideally with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.
